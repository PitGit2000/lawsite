About Golo Law Office

Established in 1993, Golo Law Office has built a reputation as a trusted legal partner in Matalom, Leyte and surrounding communities. For nearly three decades, we have provided comprehensive legal services with integrity, dedication, and personalized attention.

Under the leadership of Atty. Emmanuel G. Golo, our practice has grown to serve clients across a broad spectrum of legal needs, from civil and criminal litigation to administrative proceedings and election cases.

We pride ourselves on our commitment to excellence, ethical practice, and achieving favorable outcomes for our clients. Our approach combines deep legal knowledge with a genuine understanding of our clients' needs and concerns.

Attorney Profile

Atty. Emmanuel "Maning" Golo is a seasoned attorney with over 32 years of practice in civil and criminal litigation, alternative dispute resolution, and a broad range of legal fields. Admitted to the Bar in 1989, he combines deep courtroom experience—with countless of cases in trial and appellate courts—with advisory roles and academic contributions.

Core Expertise

  • Litigation & Advocacy: Criminal and civil trials, electoral protests, administrative disputes.
  • Consulting & ADR: Counsel to the Diocese of Maasin since 2015; arbitration and mediation.
  • Specializations: Labor & employment, family law, real estate, wills & succession, local government & election law.

Academic Roles

  • Lecturer in obligations & contracts at Bato Institute (2020–21)
  • Instructor in taxation, labor, civil, international, administrative and environmental law across various institutions.

Education & Credentials

  • LL.B./J.D., University of San Carlos, 1988
  • Passed the Bar on first attempt, 1989
  • Ongoing MCLE participation to stay current on legal developments.
